Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026  ·  Effective immediately  ·  Applies globally

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of VANDL Health Score (VHS) worldwide. The Service is operated by 上海裔陇生物科技有限公司 (Shanghai Yilongsheng Biotechnology Co., Ltd.). Contact: hello@vandlvhs.com

1. Who We Are

VANDL Health Score (VHS) is a wellness education and health management platform accessible at vandlvhs.com. We are operated by 上海裔陇生物科技有限公司. We use artificial intelligence to analyze submitted health documents and generate wellness assessments for adults aged 18 and over.

2. Age Restriction — Strictly 18+

No data from persons under 18 This Service is strictly for users aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect, process, or store personal data from individuals under 18. If we discover we have collected data from a minor, we will delete it within 48 hours. To report data belonging to a minor, email hello@vandlvhs.com immediately.

3. Data We Collect

We collect only the minimum data necessary (data minimization principle):

  • Identity data: Name, age, gender (voluntarily entered)
  • Contact data: Phone number (optional)
  • Health data: Symptoms, clinical notes, uploaded health documents — classified as sensitive/special category data
  • Assessment results: AI-generated VHS score, risk profile, wellness recommendations
  • Technical data: IP address, timestamp, document language detected
  • Age verification: A timestamp in your browser's local storage confirming 18+ (stored locally only, expires in 30 days)

We do not collect: payment information, government IDs, biometric data, precise location, browsing history, or advertising data.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Generating AI-assisted wellness assessments
  • Maintaining assessment records for authorized administrators
  • Improving Service accuracy and reliability
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Detecting and preventing fraud and abuse

We do not sell, rent, share, or use your data for advertising, profiling, or marketing under any circumstances.

5. AI Processing Disclosure

AI-Generated Content (EU AI Act Article 50) All wellness assessment reports are generated by artificial intelligence. The Help Assistant is an AI chatbot — not a human. AI-generated content is clearly labelled on all results pages. VANDL VHS is a limited-risk AI system under the EU AI Act.

6. Third-Party AI Processing (Anthropic)

This Service uses Anthropic's Claude AI. When you submit a document:

  • Content is transmitted to Anthropic's API servers for processing
  • Anthropic processes this under their own Privacy Policy (anthropic.com/privacy)
  • Data may be processed in the United States
  • EU/EEA users: transfers to Anthropic are covered by Standard Contractual Clauses

By using this Service, you explicitly consent to this third-party AI processing of your health data.

7. Data Storage and Security

Assessment records are stored in MongoDB Atlas (encrypted at rest and in transit). Security measures include HTTPS/TLS, password-protected admin access with brute-force lockout, rate limiting, Content Security Policy, and input sanitization. No system is 100% secure. In the event of a breach likely to risk your rights, we will notify relevant authorities within 72 hours.

8. Data Retention

Records are retained until deletion is requested or the Service ceases operation. Deletion requests are processed within 30 days.
