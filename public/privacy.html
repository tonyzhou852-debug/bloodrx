Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026  ·  Effective immediately

BloodRx takes patient data privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

1. Who We Are

BloodRx is an AI-assisted clinical decision support tool. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information submitted through the Service at bloodrx.onrender.com.

2. Data We Collect

When you use BloodRx, we may collect the following information:

  • Patient information: Name, age, gender, phone number (as entered by the clinician)
  • Clinical data: Chief complaint, clinical notes, lab values, blood report documents
  • Analysis results: AI-generated clinical summaries and recommendations
  • Technical data: IP address, timestamp of analysis

We do NOT collect payment information, passwords, or government ID numbers.

3. How We Use Your Data

Data submitted to BloodRx is used exclusively for:

  • Generating AI-assisted clinical analysis and recommendations
  • Maintaining a record of analyses for the operating clinician
  • Improving the reliability and accuracy of the Service
  • Complying with legal obligations

We do NOT sell, rent, or share patient data with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party AI Processing

BloodRx uses Anthropic's Claude AI to process clinical data. When you submit a blood report for analysis:

  • Document content and patient information is transmitted to Anthropic's API
  • Anthropic's own privacy policy and data handling practices apply to this transmission
  • You should review Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy

By using this Service, you acknowledge and consent to this data processing.

5. Data Storage & Security

Analysis records are stored on secure servers. We implement the following security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Password-protected admin access
  • Input sanitization to prevent injection attacks
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Security headers to prevent common web attacks

However, no system is 100% secure. You use this Service at your own risk.

6. Data Retention

Analysis records are retained for operational purposes. Records may be deleted upon request. Note that data stored on the free hosting tier may be lost if the service restarts.

7. Clinician Responsibility

The clinician using this Service is responsible for:

  • Obtaining appropriate patient consent before entering their data
  • Ensuring compliance with local data protection laws (HIPAA, GDPR, etc.)
  • Maintaining appropriate confidentiality obligations
  • Not entering unnecessary sensitive patient information

8. HIPAA Notice

BloodRx is NOT a HIPAA-compliant platform. Healthcare professionals in the United States should not enter Protected Health Information (PHI) into this Service unless they have ensured appropriate safeguards are in place. Use of this Service with PHI is at the clinician's own legal risk.

9. GDPR Rights (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us through the Service.

10. Children's Privacy

This Service is not directed at individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us through the Service at bloodrx.onrender.com.
